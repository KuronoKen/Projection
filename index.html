Welcome to "Projection"

This game is an MMORPG Open World game using abilities from popular Anime, a game in the platform of Roblox. This game consists of both PVP and PVE, with a large variety of stuff to do such as Mission, Mining, Loot Hunting, Dungeon, Hanging Out, and more. The game also plans to have a Dungeon Crawler element in the future. (Currently work in progress)

Genre

3D, Multiplayer, RPG, Open-World, Adventure, Dungeon Crawler

Rating

Ages 13+ Violence (Moderate/Repeated), Blood (Realistic/Light)
